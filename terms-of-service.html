1. Introduction & Agreement

Godspeed Bulldogs is a premium English Bulldog breeding operation based in Houma, Louisiana, specializing in health-tested, AKC registered bulldogs from proven bloodlines.

2. Puppy Purchase Agreement

2.1 Waitlist & Deposit

To reserve a puppy, you must join our waitlist and submit a non-refundable deposit of $1,000. This deposit secures your position on the waitlist and will be applied toward the final purchase price.

2.2 Pricing & Payment

Puppy prices range from $6,500 to $8,500 depending on gender, color, and lineage. Final payment is due before the puppy reaches 8 weeks of age. Accepted payment methods include bank transfer, Zelle, and cash.

2.3 Puppy Selection

Puppy selection occurs at 4-6 weeks of age based on waitlist order and buyer preferences. We reserve the right to retain puppies from any litter for our breeding program.

2.4 Pick-Up & Transfer

Puppies are released at 8 weeks of age minimum. Buyers are responsible for arranging pick-up in Houma, Louisiana. Shipping arrangements may be available for an additional fee with approved transporters.

3. Health Guarantee

3.1 Two-Year Genetic Health Guarantee

We provide a 2-year health guarantee against life-threatening genetic defects. This guarantee covers:

  • Severe hip dysplasia requiring surgery
  • Severe heart defects present at birth
  • Life-threatening liver or kidney defects
  • Other congenital defects deemed life-threatening by a licensed veterinarian

3.2 Guarantee Requirements

To maintain the health guarantee, you must:

  • Have your puppy examined by a licensed veterinarian within 72 hours of pick-up
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedules
  • Maintain puppy at a healthy weight
  • Provide proper nutrition and care
  • Notify us immediately of any health concerns
Important: The health guarantee does not cover:
  • Injuries, accidents, or environmental factors
  • Conditions caused by improper care or nutrition
  • Minor conditions that do not affect quality of life
  • Cosmetic defects (underbite, overbite, loose tail, etc.)
  • Cherry eye, entropion, or other correctable conditions

3.3 Remedy

In the event of a covered genetic defect, we will offer a replacement puppy of equal value when one becomes available, OR a refund of the purchase price (minus deposit) at our discretion. Veterinary bills are not covered.

4. Breeding Rights & Spay/Neuter

All puppies are sold on LIMITED AKC registration unless otherwise agreed in writing. Puppies sold on limited registration must be spayed or neutered by 18 months of age. Proof of spay/neuter must be provided to maintain health guarantee.

Breeding rights are available for select puppies at an additional cost. Contact us directly to discuss breeding rights.

5. Return & Rehoming Policy

If at any time you can no longer care for your Godspeed Bulldog, you must contact us first. We retain the right of first refusal and will assist in rehoming if necessary. Under no circumstances should a Godspeed Bulldog be surrendered to a shelter or rescue without our knowledge.

We do not offer refunds for buyer's remorse or change of circumstances after the puppy has been in your possession for more than 72 hours.
